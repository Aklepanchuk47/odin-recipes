Perfect Pasta

About:

This "Perfect Pasta" recipe is perfect, because it contains 4 simple ingredients you can find at any grocery store or even around your kitchen. These ingredients are cheap (enter inflation) and easy for a beginner to identify. Alone, the ingredients look crude and borderline depressing. However, when combined, the ingredients become the Perfect Pasta!

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz pasta noodles (fettuccine or spaghetti noodles preferable)
  • 1 1/2 cups parmesan cheese (grated parmesan cheese preferable, trust)
  • 8 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon pepper

Steps:

  1. Bring well-salted water to a boil over high heat in a large pot. Add pasta and cook until al dente. Drain, reserving the water for later use. Set the pasta aside.
  2. Melt butter in the same pot. Add pepper and toast over medium heat for 1 minute stirring frequently.
  3. Remove from the heat. Slowly add ½ of the Parmesan, whisking continuously to incorporate into the butter.
  4. Add ½ cup reserved pasta water and the remaining Parmesan. Whisk until combined.
  5. Add pasta to the sauce and toss. If the pasta seems too dry, add reserved water ¼ cup at a time until desired consistency is reached.
  6. Serve warm, with a green salad and a side of crusty bread.
